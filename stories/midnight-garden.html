The Midnight Garden

The garden only existed between midnight and three in the morning.

Luca discovered it from his kitchen window where cracked concrete should have been.

The Rules

Inside the garden, time stretched, stars were unfamiliar, and every flower carried someone else's memory.

Touching bark revealed moments from lives he had never lived.

The Gardener

A woman tending white roses told him the garden made room for memories people feared in daylight.

At three o'clock it vanished back into concrete, waiting for the next night.
